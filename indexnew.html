Support Your Local Masjid

Our mosque is at the heart of the community — a place of prayer, learning, and support for all. To continue providing a welcoming and safe environment, we rely on the generosity of our community to help cover essential running costs such as electricity, water, heating, maintenance, and cleaning.

Every donation, no matter the amount, helps keep the doors of the mosque open and supports the services we provide for daily prayers, Jumu’ah, classes, and community events.

If you are able, please consider donating to support your local mosque. May Allah reward you for your generosity and grant barakah in your wealth.
